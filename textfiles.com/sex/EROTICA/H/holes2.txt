..BEFORE THE BOYS LEFT THEY ALL STOOD OVER ME AND PISSED ALL OVER ME I
REALLY FELT HUMILIATED.AFTER THEY HAD LEFT I MANAGED TO WALK OVER TO MY
WIFE WHO DESPITE THE TORTURE AND RAPE HAD A BIG SMILE ON HER FACE,WE REALISED
THE CAMERA HAD GONE AND WE WERE CONCERNED ABOUT SOME FORM OF BLACKMAIL,THE
LETTER CAME 2 DAYS LATER.

THE LEADER OF THE BOYS HAD BEEN INITIATED INTO THIS BY HIS FATHER AND HAD
SHOWN HIM THE TAPE,THE BOY WAS ACTUALLY MORE INTERESTED IN ME AND HE
SUGGESTED A DEAL WHICH INCLUDED HIS FATHER.THIS BOY HAD INHERITED OVER
200.MILLION USD FROM HIS UNCLE AND THE FATHER WAS HIS GUARDIAN UNTIL HE
WAS 22YRS OLD,THE NEXT YEAR IN FACT,IN THE MEAN TIME THE FATHER HAD
TAUGHT HIM ALL HE KNEW.

MY WIFE WAS INVITED TO THEIR HOUSE AND SHE TOLD ME TO STAY AND I REALISED
SHE ENJOYED THE SLAVERY OF ME GETTING HER OWN BACK IN THE PROCESS,SHE WAS
AWAY ALMOST 4HRS AND RETURNED WITH DETAILS OF WHAT WE COULD DO,SHE WAS
TO GO ALL EXPENSES PAID FIRST CLASS WITH THE FATHER TO A GREEK ISLAND
THAT THE FAMILY HAD OWNED FOR YEARS AND I WAS TO STAY HERE IN LONDON
BUT AT THE SERVICE OF THE BOY MICHAEL TO DO WITH WHATEVER HE WANTED.

THE DAY CAME FOR MY WIFE TO GO AND SHE STOOD DRESSED IN THE SHORTEST
SKIRT POSSIBLE WITH HIGH HEELS AND OPEN TOES BEAUTIFULLY PAINTED BRIGHT
PINK,A LARGE ANKLE CHAIN ADORNED HER RIGHT ANKLE,HER NAILS WERE VERY
LONG AND PAINTED AND SHE WORE A RING ON EACH FINGER AND LOTS OF BRACELETS,
SHE WORE A SHORT LOW CUT TOP AND A GREAT DEAL OF MAKEUP AND LOOKED THE
IDEAL SLUT!

SHE EXPLAINED HOW WE WE REALLY WELL OFF AND WE SHOULD ENJOY OURSELVES AND SHE
HAD AGREED TO LET ANYTHING HAPPEN WITHIN THE GUIDELINES SHE HAD WRITTEN DOWN
A COPY OF WHICH SHE LEFT WITH HER LAWYER.AS FOR ME?WELL SHE SAID THAT AFTER
YEARS OF BEING MY SLAVE IT WAS ONLY FAIR I WAS GIVEN A TASTE OF MY OWN
MEDICONE,SHE WAS SUPPOSEDLY AWAY FOR 3 WEEKS BUT JUST SAID SHE WOULD SEE
ME AFTER A LITTLE WHILE LONGER AND KISSED MY CHEEK AND LEFT.

WELL NONONE CONTACTED ME FOR OVER A WEEK EXCEPT MY WIFE TO SAY SHE WAS THERE
ON THE ISLAND AND HAD ARRIVED SAFELY AND IT WAS HOT,THEN SHE JUST HUNG UP.

THE 2ND WEEK I GOT A CALL AND WAS GIVEN AN ADDRESS TO GO TO IN SOMERSET,IT
TURNED OUT TO BE A BIG FARM SET ON ITS OWN IN ACRES AND ACRES OF WILDERNESS.
THE FARMHOUSE WAS ENORMOUS AND THE DOOR WAS OPENED BY AN INDIAN GIRL IN SAREE
AND WITH A RING THROUGH HER NOSE,SHE MUST BE THE SERVANT I THOUGHT.IWAS SHOWN
INTO THE SITTING ROOM AND WAS MET BY MICHAEL TOGETHER WITH THE 2 BLOND BOYS
THAT HAD BEEN AT MY HOUSE.

"SIT DOWN ON THE FLOOR "HE SAID"YOUR WIFE HAS LEFT INSTRUCTIONS FOR YOU AND
THE FIRST WEEK WILL BE HERE THEN YOU MAY GO HOME WAIT FOR 3 WEEKS THEN WE
WILL ARRANGE FOR YOU TO JOIN HER IN THE SUN"HERE YOU WILL DO EVERYTHING
YOU ARE TOLD AND ALL IS ARRANGED"

I WAS TAKEN UPSTAIRS AND STRIPPED AND BATHED BY THE MAID WHO THEN KISSED
ME HARD AND TO MY AMAZEMENT FUCKED MY ASS WITH THE BIGGEST COCK I HAD SEEN!
AFTER SHE OR HE HAD COME,I FELL ASLEEP AND AWAOKE AFTER WHAT MUST HAVE BEEN
A DRUGGED SLEEP TO FIND MYSELF IN WHAT LOOKED LIKE A HOSPITAL BED AND HELD
IN BY LEATHER RESTRAINTS.A NURSE ENTERED AND BEFORE I COULD OBJECT INJECTED
ME WITH SOMETHING LIKE A PRE-MED AS I FLOATED ON THE EDGE OF CONCIOUSNESS,
A MAN IN A WHITE OVERALL CAME IN AND ANNOUNCED HE WAS THE SURGEON I COULD NOT
MAKE MYSELF ASK BUT HE SAVED ME THE TROUBLE.HE EXPLAINED MY WIFE WANTED
ME TO BE ABLE TO ENJOY ALL FORMS OF SEX STILL BE MANLY BUT BE ABLE TO BE
FEMININE,HE WOULD NOW ELECTROLYSE ALL MY BODY HAIR SO IT WOULD GROW SLOWLY
AND AT THE SAME TIME CARRY OUT SOME LIPOSUCTION REDISTIBUTING MY BODY FAT
AND "ONE OR TWO OTHER THINGS!"

FOR TWO DAYS AFTER THE OPERATION THEY KEPT ME SEDATED AND I REALLY DID NOT
KNOW WHAT HAD HAPPENED,FINALLY IWAS ALLOWED TO EXPLORE MY OWN BODY,TRUE I
WAS SMOOTH AND EVEN MY BEARD HAD GONE,MY CHEST WAS ACTUALLY SMALLER BUT I HAD
DISTINCT SMALL BREASTS WHICH ON THEIR OWN LOOKED MASCULINE BUT WHEN PUSHED
UP WITH THE HANDS OR I GUESSED A BRA THEY FORMED A RESPECTABLE CLEAVAGE.

MY FINGERNAILS WERE SHORT BUT IMMACULATE,AND MY EAR LOBES HAD BEEN PIERCED
AND SMALL RINGS PUT IN HARDLY NOTICEABLE ALL IN ALL NOT BAD AND BETTER THAN
I HAD FEARED.

I LEFT THE CLINIC AND WENT HOME ANSWERING MAIL AND GENERALLY PUTTING THINGS
IN ORDER UNTIL THE CAR CAME TO TAKE ME TO THE AIRPORT.

I ARRIVED AT THE ISLAND AND WAS GREETED BY MICHAELS FATHER,WHO TOLD ME THAT
APART FROM 4 FEMALE SERVANTS AND 2 GARDENERS THE ISLAND WAS QUITE DESERTED
AND PRIVATE.HE TOOK ME TO THE HOUSE AND PUT ME IN A ROOM AND THEN MY WIFE
WALKED IN I WAS STUNNED!SHE WAS TANNED AND OBVIOUSLY FIT,BUT HER EARS,ALREADY
PIERCED THREE TIMES WERE NOW SPORTING 6 EARRINGS EACH SIDE,HER NOSE WAS PIERCED
THROUGH HER SHIRT I COULD SEE HER NIPPLES ADORNED WITH RINGS,"I AM ALSO
PIERCED IN MY LABIA AND CLITORIS AND WITH ANEASTHETIC IT DID NOT HURT A BIT
AND THE SENSATION IS WONDERFUL,SHE TOOK HER HAT OFF AND REVEALED THAT SHE
HAD HAD HER HEAD SHAVED AND EVEN HER EYEBROWS HAD GONE JUST HER LONG EYELASHES
REMAINING THE EFFECT WAS MOST DRAMATIC.SHE HAD A HARNESS ON AROUND HER WAIST
AND SHE EXPLAINED THAT SHE HAD AGREED TO HAVE HER ASSHOLE STRECHED MEDICALLY
TO ALLOW INTERCOURSE AND WITH MEDICATION IT HAD NOT HURT A BIT.

THEN I WAS TAKEN DOWNSTAIRS AND BEFORE I COULD OBJECT THROWN INTO A SMALL
ROOM WITH JUST A HOLE IN THE CORNER AND TOLD TO STRIP,THIS TIME I RELALISED T
TORTURE WOULD BE FOR REAL WITH NO SEDATION.......CONT HOLES3
