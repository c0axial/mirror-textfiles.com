 THE WIFE'S ORDEAL BY GANG BANG AND SUBSEQUENT PUNISHMENT
 --------------------------------------------------------

 BY  MIT RETSOF  3/4/92


 CHAPTER 1 - "THE HOLES ARE FILLED!"
 ----------------------------------


MY WIFE HAD BEEN ENJOYING HER LIFE OF DOMESTICITY OF LATE NOT HAVING
TO WORK AS MY BUSINESS WAS GOING WELL,SHE STAYED IN BED TILL 11AM MOST
MORNINGS AND THEN SPENT THE DAY SHOPPING OR GOING TO THE BEAUTY SALON.

SHE LIKED BEING MY SLAVE AND DRESSED TO ORDER,SHE HAD LONG FINGERNAILS
ABOUT 1 INCH ON EACH AND HAD PAINTED THEM A SLUTTISH PINK,HER BLOND
HAIR WAS LONG AND SHE HAD TAKEN TO WEARING "PUNK" STYLE MAKE-UP VERY
THICKLY APPLIED.SHE HAD GREAT TITS AND THE REST OF HER WAS V.OK BY ME
HIGH HEELS WERE OBLIGATORY AND AS IT WAS SUMMER OPENED TOED 5 INCH
HEELS AND I HAD MADE HER GROW HER TOENAILS WHICH ALSO WERE PAINTED
BRIGHT PINK.

PAIN HAD ALWAYS BEEN AN EXCITING PROSPECT FOR HER AND WITH THE SUMMER
COMING GOOD AT LAST I WAS DETERMINED TO TAKE ADVANTAGE,OUR SAILBOAT
BECKONED AND I HAD SOME IDEAS TO PUT INTO PRACTICE,BUT FIRST AN
OPPORTUNITY PRESENTED ITSELF WHICH WAS TOO GOOD TO MISS AND I WENT TO
WORK WITH A JOY IN MY HEART AND A LUMP IN MY CROTCH!

WE HAD ALWAYS BEEN OFFERED THE USE OF CASUAL LABOUR FOR OUR GARDEN
WHICH WAS BIG AND SECLUDED OUR HOUSE BEING IN A WOODED SECTION OF SOME
WILD COMMON LAND,USUALLY THE LABOUR WAS STUDENTS AND WE HAD TO OFFER
FOOD AND SHELTER IN OUR LARGE BARN IN RETURN FOR THE GARDEN BEING
CLEARED AND GENERALLY PUT IN ORDER FOR THE SUMMER.

THAT YEAR 7 BOYS SHOWED UP HAVING BEEN SENT FROM THE SCHOOL ABOUT 30
MILES AWAY,THEY WERE ALL AROUND 16YRS OLD AND THEY STARTED WORKING
HARD.AFTER A COUPLE OF DAYS WE HAD STRUCK A FRIENDSHIP AND THEY WOULD
JOIN US FOR DINNER AND A DRINK.

MY WIFE WAS TURNED ON BY THEM AND HAD SAID SO MUCH TO ME,THEY HAD BEEN
WITH US A WEEK WHEN ONE FRIDAY EVENING THEY HAD HAD  A LOT OF WINE AND
WERE FAIRLY MERRY,I DISCOVERED THEY HAD BEEN LOOKING AT SOME OF MY
VIDEOS IN THE SUMMER HOUSE ONE OR TWO WHICH WERE HOMEMADE OF MY
WIFE,AFTER MUCH TALK I PERSUADED THEM TO JOIN IN WITH A SCENARIO,AND
THE STAGE WAS SET.

MY WIFE WENT OUT TO THE BARN IN HER BATH ROBE TO TAKE HER BAG FROM THE
CAR,I WAS ALREADY IN PLACE WITH THE CAMERA AND AS SHE CAME IN THE BOYS
POUNCED.

SHE DID NOT STRUGGLE AND SOON ALL 7 WERE NAKED,SHE WAS SUCKING AND
TOUCHING EVERYTHING SHE COULD LAY HER HANDS ON,THEY PUSHED HER ONTO
THE FLOOR AND STARTED ONE BY ONE TO FUCK HER,WHEN ONE WAS FINISHED HE
WOULD MOVE TO HER SIDE AND LET HER LICK HIM CLEAN.MY HANDS WERE FAR
FROM STEADY AS I FILMED EVERYTHING,WHEN THEY HAD FINISHED THEY LOOKED
UP WHERE I WAS AND I GAVE THEM THE OK TO GO A STAGE FURTHER.

MY WIFE HAD NEVER BEEN FUCKED IN THE ASS AND WAS ALWAYS SCARED BUT
THEY JUST TURNED HER OVER AND WITH HER ASS AND THIGHS LUBRICATED WITH
THEIR COME I WATCHED AS THEY EACH IN TURN IGNORING HER SHOUTS AND
SCREAMS,PROCEEDED TO RAM THEIR BY NOW ROCK HARD AGAIN DICKS UP HER
ASSHOLE,THE FIRST ONE FINISHED AND WITHDREW HIS DICK WHITE AND BROWN
AND HE WENT STRAIGHT TO HER FACE AND FORCED HER TO LICK HIM CLEAN!

WHEN THEY HAD FINISHED SHE LAY THERE AND THEY MOVED OUT OF THE BARN,I
WENT DOWN THE CAMERA STILL RUNNING AS I TOOK IN HER ASS OPEN AND WIDE
AND COVERED IN CUM.

SHE SMILED AND SAID SHE HAD ENJOYED IT SHE FELT HUMILIATED AND ABUSED
AND WOULD THEY DO MORE?I FELT THINGS HAD GONE FAR ENOUGH BUT SAID
MAYBE WE COULD EXPERIENCE THE SAME AGAIN SOME TIME,BUT LITTLE DID I
KNOW WHAT PANDORA'S BOX I HAD OPENED UP.


CHAPTER 2 "THE PAIN OR THE PLEASURE?"
-------------------------------------

IT WAS ABOUT A FORTNIGHT AFTER THE "BANG"AND WE HAD BEEN AWAY IN
FRANCE AND LEFT THE BOYS TO GET ON WITH IT,SHE HAD NEVER FELT SO
EXCITED AND SHE ADMITTED THAT HER FANATSY HAD ACTUALLY BEEN TO SEE ME
WITH ONE OF THE BOYS,THIS SURPRISED ME I HAVE NEVER CONSIDERED MYSELF
GAY IN ANY SHAPE OR FORM OTHER THAN WHEN I WAS AT SCHOOL AT THE AGE OF
12 I HAD BEEN FORCED TO SUCK TWO BOYS AND ONE OF THEM HAD BUGGERED ME.
I HAD TOLD HER THIS AND IT OBVIOUSLY WAS THE ROOT OF HER FANATASY.

THAT EVENING WE WERE LYING IN BED READING AND I FELL ASLEEP,ONLY TO BE
WOKEN UP BY A RAG OR PAD BEING FORCED OVER MY FACE,THE ACID SMELL OF
CHLORYLL FILLED MY HEAD AND I DRIFTED AWAY.

I CAME TO FEELING SICK,AND I REALISED I WAS IN THE BARN TIED TO
A CHAIR,THE OLDER OF THE BOYS CAME OVER AND SAID THEY HAD TIME TO
CONSIDER EVERYTHING AND THEY LIKED US BOTH AND FELT THEY WANTED TO
TAKE THE INTIATIVE,I STARTED TO ARGUE AND A GAG WAS  FORCED INTO MY
MOUTH.

I COULD NOT MOVE OR TAKE MY EYES OFF MY WIFE WHO WAS SUSPENDED WITH
HER FEET JUST TOUCHING THE FLOOR AND LEGS WIDE APART.THEY MADE ME
WATCH WHILE THEY WHIPPED HER VERY HARD SHE WAS SCREAMING AND SHOUTING
AS THE MARKS GREW BIGGER AND THE BRUISES CAME.THEY TIED STRING TO HER
NIPPLES AND HUNG TWO BRICKS STRECHING HER BREASTS,THEY THEN CLIMBED
ONTO A FRAME BESIDE HER  AND ALL 7 OF THEM MASTURBATED OVER HER FACE
COVERING HER HAIR AND BREASTS WITH COME THAT DRIPPED OFF HER NOSE AND
LIPS.

THEY LEFT HER AND TO MY SURPRISE CAME OVER TO ME AND WITHOUT FURTHER
TO DO UNTIED ME,REMOVED THE GAG AND HELD ME DOWN,THEY FORCED ME TO
LICK EACH ONES ASSHOLE IN TURN FORCING ME TO PUT MY TONGUE INTO THE
HOLE,I WAS EXCITED BY THIS TIME AND WHEN TWO OF THEM ACTUALLY FARTED
INTO MY MOUTH I DID NOT CARE.

I FELT SUDDENLY MY ASSHOLE BEING OPENED AND A BURNING SENSATION AS THE
FIRST BOY DRILLED MY BACK PASSAGE,AS THEY HAD DONE WITH MY WIFE EACH
ONE INTURN MADE ME LICK HIM CLEAN,FINALLY THEY LET ME STAND AND UNTIED
MY WIFE,SHE WAS SMILING AND I COULD FEEL THE COME DRIPPING DOWN MY
LEGS,THE BOYS WERE GRINNING AND LAUGHING WHAT NEXT I THOUGHT COULD
POSSIBLY HAPPEN?STOP THE CAMERA BEFORE YOU LEAVE ONE SHOUTED!......
