

                        AN INTERESTING SATURDAY NIGHT
                                 BY: ESSEN M.
                            EDITED BY: MASTER WADE


       MY DAY BEGAN NORMALLY AS A USUAL SATURDAY, BUT I KNEW THAT I WAS GOING
   TO ENTERTAIN AT LEAST TWO COUPLES THAT NIGHT.  SO I WENT AHEAD AND GOT THE
   LITTLE TASKS OUT OF THE WAY THAT I ALWAYS DO, AND THEN TOOK A COUPLE HOURS
   OFF TO RELAX.  BUT ABOUT TWO THIRTY I DECIDED THAT IT WAS ABOUT TIME THAT
   I STARTED GETTING READY FOR THE EVENING.

       THE FIRST THING THAT I KNEW I HAD TO DO WAS GO INTO THE BATHROOM AND
   START GETTING MYSELF CLEANED OUT.  I USUALLY START THE NIGHT BEFORE OR NO
   LATER THAN BREAKFAST BY NOT EATING ANYTHING PAST THAT POINT.  THEN I WILL
   TAKE A LIGHT LAXATIVE IN THE MORNING TO HELP WITH WHAT I HAVE TO DO LATER
   ON IN THE DAY.  EVERYTHING SO FAR HAD WORKED AS PLANNED AND I WAS FEELING
   REAL EMPTY BY NOW, AND EVEN A BIT HUNGRY.  BUT I KNEW THAT IF I ATE
   ANYTHING I WOULD PAY FOR IT LATER, AND I PUT THE HUNGER OUT OF MY MIND.
   WELL, I FILLED MY OLD BAG UP TO ABOUT A QUART AND A HALF, AND HUNG IT ON
   THE SIDE OF THE SHOWER.  THE WHITE TUBE AND TIP HUNG TO THE FLOOR, SO I
   GOT MYSELF DOWN AND TURNED ON MY SIDE.  I REACHED AROUND AND GOT THE TIP
   IN MY HAND AND SLIPPED IT INSIDE ME.  ALL I NEEDED TO DO WAS SLIDE MY HAND
   UP AND RELEASE THE CLAMP NOW. ONCE I DID THAT I JUST LAID BACK AND RELAXED
   WHILE I FELT THE WARM WATER GOING INSIDE ME.  IT REALLY FELT GOOD TO ME AS
   I AM ACCUSTOMED TO HAVING ENEMAS ON A REGULAR BASIS. I MADE THE MOST OF
   THE FEELING.  IT DOES NOT TAKE LONG FOR JUST A QUART AND A HALF TO RUN
   OUT, AND THIS TIME WAS NO DIFFERENT. WELL AFTER IT WAS EMPTY I TOOK THE
   TIP OUT AND LAID IT ON A TOWEL BESIDE ME.  AFTER THAT I JUST LAID ON MY
   BACK AND LET THE WATER DO ITS THING.  THE FEELING OF THE WATER MOVING
   AROUND INSIDE ME AND GOING FROM PLACE TO PLACE IS SOMETHING YOU HAVE TO
   FEEL TO UNDERSTAND.  AS MUCH AS I ENJOY IT I HAVE NEVER BEEN ABLE TO
   EXPLAIN JUST HOW IT FEELS SO OTHERS CAN UNDERSTAND.

       WELL, TIME PASSED, AND I KNEW THAT THE WATER HAD DONE ITS THING AND I
   NEEDED TO GET UP AND GO SIT ON THE JOHN.  I HAVE LEARNED AND TAUGHT MYSELF
   TO COMPLETELY RELAX AS THAT MAKES EXPELLING THE WATER MUCH EASIER.  IT WAS
   NO DIFFERENT THIS TIME BECAUSE AS I RELAXED MORE AND MORE, IT ALL CAME
   OUT.  I NOTICED THAT IT WAS NOT AS CLEAR AS I NEEDED IT TO BE SO I KNEW
   THAT I WOULD NEED AT LEAST ONE MORE.  I USUALLY WAIT AT LEAST THIRTY
   MINUTES BETWEEN ENEMAS AS THAT HAS PROVEN TO BE A BETTER METHOD FOR
   CLEANING MYSELF OUT.  BUT WHEN MY BROTHER DOES IT FOR ME HE WILL GIVE ME
   ONE RIGHT AFTER THE OTHER.  I GET CLEAN THAT WAY, BUT IT IS NOT AS
   COMFORTABLE FOR ME!

       ABOUT THIRTY MINUTES HAD PASSED AND I WENT BACK AND REPEATED THE
   PROCESS AGAIN.  IT WAS GETTING CLOSE TO FOUR IN THE AFTERNOON NOW AND I
   HAD TO TRY AND BE FINISHED BY FIVE IF I COULD.  THE PLACE THAT I WAS GOING
   WAS JUST OVER SIXTY MILES AWAY AND I HAD TO BE THERE NO LATER THAN SEVEN.
   SO I RUSHED THIS ONE SOME AND CHECKED TO MAKE SURE THAT IT WAS EITHER
   CLEAR OR ALMOST CLEAR.  IT WAS!!  I DIDN'T BOTHER GETTING DRESSED YET AND
   JUST WALKED INTO THE LIVING ROOM WHERE MY BROTHER WAS  WATCHING WRESTLING
   ON TV.  I TOLD HIM THAT I HAD CLEANED MYSELF OUT AS BEST AS I COULD AND
   ASKED IF HE WANTED TO CHECK.  WELL, HE TOLD ME TO  GET TWO FLEET READY
   ENEMAS AND BRING THEM BACK.  WE HAVE ALWAYS KEPT SEVERAL OF THOSE ON HAND
   AS THEY ARE GOOD TO HAVE FOR A FINAL CLEANSING.  I RETURNED WITH THEM
   STILL IN THEIR GREEN AND WHITE BOXES AND HANDED THEM TO MY BROTHER.  HE
   TOLD ME TO GET ON MY KNEES WITH MY HEAD DOWN TO THE FLOOR IN FRONT OF HIM.
   JUST AS I DID I FELT THE FIRST TIP GO INSIDE ME AND THE LIQUID RUN IN.
   JUST A FEW SECONDS LATER I FELT THE OTHER TIP INSIDE ME AND THE LIQUID
   FROM THE OTHER ONE WAS INSERTED.  HE TOLD ME TO FLIP OVER ON MY BACK AND
   HE STARTED RUBBING MY STOMACH.  DAMN, THAT ALWAYS MAKES IT HARDER TO HOLD,
   BUT IT DOES MAKE SURE THAT THERE IS NOTHING LEFT IN ME.  AFTER I WAS
   REALLY STRAINING TO HOLD IT HE TOLD ME TO GO AND EXPEL IT, BUT NOT TO
   FLUSH.  I WAS GLAD TO OBEY HIM AT THIS POINT AND HEADED FOR THE JOHN.

       I GOT IN THERE JUST IN TIME, AND I MEAN WITH OUT A SECOND TO SPARE.
   SO I JUST RELAXED AND IT DIDN'T TAKE LONG FOR IT TO ALL COME OUT.  THIS
   TIME IT WAS ONLY LIQUID AND THAT DOES MAKE EXPULSION MUCH FASTER.  WHEN I
   WAS FINISHED I CALLED MY BROTHER AS I KNEW HE WANTED TO BE SURE THAT I WAS
   AS CLEAN AS I COULD BE AT THIS POINT, AND I MIGHT ADD...I WAS. WELL, AFTER
   HE WAS SATISFIED THAT THE TASK WAS COMPLETED HE TOLD ME TO TAKE A QUICK
   SHOWER AND JUST SLIP ON A DRESS (NO PANTIES OR BRA, JUST A DRESS).

       I DRIED OFF AND WENT TO MY ROOM AND PICKED OUT A LOOSE FITTING DRESS
   AND SLIPPED IT ON.  THEN I WALKED OUT INTO THE LIVING ROOM WHERE MY
   BROTHER WAS STILL WATCHING WRESTLING. HE ASKED IF I WAS READY AND I SAID,
   "YES!"  SO HE GOT UP AND TOLD ME TO SLIP ON A PAIR OF FLATS AND WE HEADED
   FOR THE VAN.  WE HAD ABOUT AN HOUR  DRIVE TO THE HOUSE WHERE I WAS
   EXPECTED.  WE TALKED ON THE WAY THERE AND I ASKED WHAT HE HAD PLANNED.  HE
   SAID THAT HE WAS JUST GOING TO DROP ME OFF AND PICK ME UP IN THE MORNING
   AROUND EIGHT OR SO.  I ASKED WHY HE WASN'T STAYING THIS TIME, AND HE TOLD
   ME THAT THEY WANTED ME ALONE.  THAT IS WHEN I ASKED WHAT THEY HAD PLANNED.
   HE REPLIED THAT HE WASN'T SURE BUT THAT IT WAS GOING TO LAST QUITE A
   WHILE.  I HAD BEEN WITH ONE OF THESE COUPLES BEFORE SO I REALLY WASN'T TOO
   WORRIED ABOUT BEING LEFT ALONE. I DIDN'T KNOW EXACTLY HOW MANY OTHERS WERE
   GOING TO BE THERE, BUT KNOWING WOULD NOT HAVE MADE ANY DIFFERENCE, AS A
   DEAL IS A DEAL, AND I AGREED TO GOING.

       EVEN THOUGH IS WAS RIGHT AT AN HOUR WE SURE SEEMED TO GET THERE FAST.
   WE ARRIVED ABOUT FIVE MINUTES BEFORE SEVEN, AND THAT MADE ME RIGHT ON
   TIME.  THEY HAD A BIG HOUSE, SET WELL BACK FROM THE ROAD. AS WE DROVE IN
   THE DRIVEWAY I SAW THE PORCH LIGHT COME ON.  WHEN WE STOPPED THE VAN, THE
   WOMAN OF THE HOUSE WAS STANDING AT THE DOOR WAITING FOR US. I GOT OUT AND
   WALKED TOWARD THE DOOR WITH MY BROTHER BEHIND ME.  AS WE GOT INSIDE THE
   ENTRANCE-WAY MY BROTHER TOLD ME TO STRIP DOWN AND TAKE OFF MY SHOES.  I
   SLIPPED MY DRESS OFF AND HANDED IT TO MY BROTHER AND THEN HE REACHED DOWN
   AND PICKED UP MY SHOES.  AT THAT POINT THE WOMAN OF THE HOUSE WAS SLIPPING
   A BELT AROUND ME WITH A BIG RING IN THE CENTER OF IT.  SHE WENT BEHIND ME
   AND BUCKLED IT TIGHT AROUND MY WAIST.  SHE CAME BACK AROUND IN FRONT OF ME
   AND TOLD ME TO STICK OUT MY RIGHT ARM.  THAT IS WHEN SHE FASTENED A
   HANDCUFF TO IT, AND PULLED IT DOWN SO SHE COULD SLIP THE OTHER CUFF
   THROUGH THE D-RING.  THEN SHE REACHED FOR MY LEFT ARM AND PUSHED IT DOWN
   WHERE SHE COULD FASTEN THE OTHER CUFF ON MY WRIST.  AFTER THAT SHE WALKED
   INTO THE NEXT ROOM AND CAME BACK WITH A PAIR OF METAL LEG CUFFS AND SHORT
   CHAIN BETWEEN THEM.  SHE GOT DOWN AND FASTENED THEM ON MY ANKLES.  ONCE
   SHE HAD ME WELL DISABLED FROM ANY SUDDEN MOVES SHE TOLD MY BROTHER THAT IF
   HE WANTED HE COULD LEAVE AND COME BACK IN THE MORNING.  SO HE REACHED FOR
   THE DOOR KNOB AND OUT HE WENT.  A FEW MINUTES LATER I WAS STILL STANDING
   IN THE ENTRANCE-WAY AND HEARD THE VAN START AND DRIVE OFF.  AT THIS POINT
   I WAS ON MY OWN, AND I MEAN ON MY OWN.

        THE WOMAN TOLD ME TO FOLLOW HER, AND WE WALKED INTO THE MAIN PART OF
   THE HOUSE.  AS I ENTERED THE NEXT ROOM THERE WAS ONLY HER HUSBAND THERE
   SITTING READING THE PAPER. I ASKED IF I WAS JUST HER SLAVE FOR THE NIGHT,
   AND SHE SAID THAT THE REST WOULD BE ARRIVING ABOUT SEVEN THIRTY.  THEN SHE
   TOLD ME THAT WAS MY ONLY QUESTION AND I WAS NOT TO SPEAK AGAIN UNLESS I
   WAS TOLD TO.  SO I STARTED HEADING FOR A CHAIR TO SIT AND WAIT, BUT THAT
   WAS NOT TO BE.  SHE TOLD ME TO GET IN THE CENTER OF THE ROOM AND STAND
   THERE UNTIL THE OTHERS ARRIVED.  I PROMPTLY DID SO, AS I WAS IN NO
   POSITION TO MADE HER UPSET WITH ME.

         ............................................................

       IT DIDN'T TAKE THAT LONG BEFORE IT SOUNDED LIKE A CAR PULLING UP
   OUTSIDE.  SHE HEARD IT ALSO AND WENT TO THE DOOR TO MEET HER GUESTS.  I
   HEARD THEM COME IN AND THEY STARTED TALKING.  ONE OF THE WOMEN THAT CAME
   ASKED IF I WAS THERE YET, AND THE LADY OF THE HOUSE SAID I WAS.  THAT SAME
   WOMAN ASKED IF I WAS READY FOR WHAT THEY HAD PLANNED, AND THE LADY OF THE
   HOUSE RESPONDED, "I'M NOT SURE, BUT I THINK WE CAN CHECK FOR OURSELVES TO
   MAKE SURE."  BY NOW THEY WERE WALKING INTO THE LIVING ROOM AND I SAW THAT
   THERE WERE TWO OTHER COUPLES.  THAT MADE SIX PEOPLE IN ALL AND I KNEW THEN
   IT WAS NOT GOING TO BE ONE OF MY BETTER NIGHTS.

       THE TWO MEN WENT OVER AND SAT DOWN AND STARTED TALKING WITH THE OWNER
   OF THE HOUSE.  NOW THE THREE WOMEN WERE ALL AROUND ME LOOKING ME OVER LIKE
   THEY WERE GOING TO BUY ME OR SOMETHING.  ONE OF THE WOMEN LOOKED AT THE
   OTHERS.

       "ARE WE READY TO START?", SHE ASKED.

        BOTH OF THEM REPLIED, "YES!"

        "OKAY", SHE SAID, "LETS GET HER IN THE DEN AND GET BUSY, WE HAVE
   THREE HORNY MEN TO MAKE HAPPY!"

        AS BEST AS I COULD, I FOLLOWED ONE OF THE WOMEN TO THE DEN WITH THE
   OTHER TWO CLOSE BEHIND ME.  WHEN WE ENTERED THE DEN THEY CLOSED THE DOOR
   BEHIND THEM.

       BY NOW I HAD A CHANCE TO LOOK AROUND THE ROOM TO SEE WHAT WAS THERE.
   IT LOOKED PRETTY MUCH LIKE ANY OTHER DEN, A DESK AND A FEW CHAIRS ALONG
   WITH A BAR IN ONE CORNER. THE ONLY THING OUT OF PLACE WAS A NARROW TABLE
   IN THE MIDDLE OF THE ROOM, BUT I KNEW THAT IT WAS JUST WHERE THEY WANTED
   IT AND THAT IT WAS GOING TO BE WELL USED; THAT I WAS SURE OF. ONE OF THE
   WOMEN GRABBED THE RING ON MY BELT AND LEAD ME OVER TOWARD THE TABLE.

       "ARE YOU READY?", SHE ASKED.

        "WHAT DO YOU MEAN?", I RESPONDED, UNSURE IF I WAS SUPPOSED TO ANSWER
   HER QUESTION.

        "DID YOU GET YOURSELF CLEANED OUT REAL GOOD BEFORE YOU LEFT HOME?"
   SHE ASKED.

        "I HAD MY LAST ENEMA ABOUT TWO HOURS AGO NOW", I REPLIED.

        SHE LOOKED OVER AT THE OTHER TWO WOMEN AND SAID, "WELL, LADIES, YOU
   KNOW WHAT WE HAVE TO DO SO LETS GET WITH IT."

       I WAS STANDING NEXT TO THE TABLE WHEN THE LADY OF THE HOUSE CAME IN
   FRONT OF ME WITH THE KEY TO THE HANDCUFFS. SHE REACHED DOWN AND TOOK THEM
   OFF OF BOTH WRISTS AND THEN SLIPPED THE BELT AROUND ME AND TOOK IT OFF.
   SHE TOLD ME TO HOP UP ON THE TABLE AND LAY ON MY STOMACH.  I DID JUST AS
   SHE SAID!  SHE TOLD ME TO SLIDE DOWN TO WHERE MY ASS WAS JUST GOING OFF
   THE BOTTOM END.  AS I WAS DOING WHAT SHE ASKED SHE HELPED ADJUST ME JUST
   WHERE SHE WANTED ME TO BE.  I WAS IN POSITION WHEN I NOTICED A WOMAN ON
   EACH SIDE OF THE TABLE WITH A LEATHER WRIST CUFF EACH.  THEY EACH TOOK AN
   ARM AND PLACED THE HEAVY LEATHER CUFF ON EACH WRIST.  THEN THEY PULLED MY
   ARMS DOWN AND FASTENED THEM TO SOMETHING ON OR NEAR THE FLOOR.  AS THIS
   WAS TAKING PLACE THE THIRD WOMAN WAS PLACING A BELT OVER ME AT MY WAIST.
   SHE SLIPPED IT INTO A BUCKLE AND DREW IT UP TIGHT.  THERE I WAS FASTENED
   TO THE TABLE FACE DOWN FROM THE WAIST UP.  AND I MEAN FASTENED, AS I TRIED
   TO RAISE UP BUT COULD ONLY MOVE AN INCH OR SO.

       AT THIS POINT I WAS NOT SURE WHAT THEY HAD PLANNED, BUT WHAT DID IT
   MATTER AS I WAS IN NO POSITION TO OBJECT.  THE LADY OF THE HOUSE WALKED
   OVER TO A DRAWER AND GOT A BALL GAG WITH STRAP OUT OF IT.  SHE TOSSED IT
   TO THE WOMAN NEAREST ME AND TOLD HER TO PUT IT ON ME.  SHE SAID WITH A
   SARCASTIC TONE TO HER VOICE "WE DON'T WANT TO DISTURB THE MEN DO WE"? SO
   SHE SLIPPED THE BALL IN MY MOUTH AS I OPENED IT FOR HER, AND FASTENED THE
   BUCKLE BEHIND MY HEAD.

       THEY ALL STOOD THERE FOR A FEW SECONDS AS IF THEY WERE ADMIRING THEIR
   HANDY-WORK.  THAT IS WHEN THE LADY OF THE HOUSE SPOKE UP AND SAID "YOU
   KNOW WHAT WE PLANNED, SO LETS GET TO IT".  SHE LOOKED AT ONE OF THE WOMEN
   AND SAID, "YOU GO OUT IN THE GARAGE AND GET THAT BASKET OF ITEMS I HAVE
   SITTING NEAR THE DOOR.  LOOKING AT THE OTHER WOMAN SHE SAID, "LETS GET
   THESE OTHER CUFFS ON HER ANKLES SO WE CAN GET HER LEGS APART."  AT THAT
   POINT I FELT HER DOWN AT MY ANKLES REMOVING THE SET OF METAL CUFFS AND
   CHAIN.  I ALSO FELT A SET OF WIDE LEATHER CUFFS BEING PLACED ON EACH
   ANKLE.  BY NOW THE WOMAN THAT WENT FOR THE BASKET WAS BACK AND IT HAD A
   LOT OF ROPES AND CABLE IN IT.  THERE WAS ALSO A MEDIUM SIZE CLOTH BAG ON
   THE TOP OF IT, AND I HAD NO IDEA WHAT IT WAS. SHE CARRIED THE BASKET OVER
   AND SAT IT DOWN ON THE FLOOR BETWEEN MY LEGS.

       I COULD HEAR ONE OF THEM GOING THROUGH THE BASKET PULLING THINGS OUT
   OF IT.  I TRIED TO LOOK AROUND, BUT DUE TO THE POSITION I WAS IN THAT
   WASN'T TOO EASY.  NEVERTHELESS, THIS DIDN'T MATTER EITHER, AS I SOON FOUND
   SOMETHING BEING FASTENED TO A RING ON EACH OF MY ANKLE CUFFS.  THEN EACH
   WOMAN WALKED AWAY FROM ME TOWARD THE WALLS ON EACH SIDE OF THE ROOM.  THEY
   PLACED WHATEVER THEY HAD IN THEIR HANDS INTO HOOKS THAT WERE ON EACH WALL.
   THE LADY OF THE HOUSE WAS STILL BEHIND ME, AND ASKED IF THEY WERE READY -
   THEY WERE.

        "OK", SHE SAID, "LETS OPEN HER UP...WIDE!" I COULD FEEL MY LEGS BEING
   DRAWN INTO THE AIR AND OUT TO EACH SIDE NOW.  SOON THEY WERE ALMOST
   STRAIGHT OUT TO EACH SIDE AND FAIRLY WIDE APART.  THE WOMAN BEHIND ME TOLD
   THE OTHERS TO KEEP PULLING UNTIL SHE SAID TO STOP, AND THEY DID.  PRETTY
   SOON MY LEGS WERE PULLED STRAIGHT OUT TO EACH SIDE AND STARTING TO HURT
   WITH THE PRESSURE.  SHE REACHED OUT AND FELT MY HIPS AND TOLD THEM THAT
   WAS JUST FINE.

       THE TWO WOMEN CAME BACK BEHIND ME AND STARTED TALKING AGAIN.  THE LADY
   OF THE HOUSE WAS DOING MOST OF THE TALKING AND BELIEVE ME I WAS REALLY
   LISTENING.

        "I'M GOING TO GIVE HER A HIGH COLONIC, AND I'LL USE SEVERAL GALLONS
   OF WATER", SHE TOLD THE OTHERS.

        "HOW ARE YOU GOING TO DO THAT WITHOUT MAKING A MESS TO CLEAN UP?",
   ONE OF THE OTHER WOMEN ASKED. JUST THEN I HEARD THE ZIPPER OF THAT BAG
   BEING OPENED.

        TAKING SOMETHING OUT OF THE BAG AND SHOWING IT TO THE OTHER WOMEN,
   SHE REPLIED, "THIS WILL TAKE CARE OF THAT PROBLEM."

        "HOW ARE YOU GOING TO GET THAT INTO HER?", ONE OF THE WOMEN ASKED.

        SHE RESPONDED SARCASTICLY, "THATS WHAT THE GAG IS FOR. I'M GOING TO
   PUSH IT INTO HER, NO MATTER WHAT IT TAKES! BESIDES, HER ASS WILL OPEN UP
   AND TAKE IT, EVEN IS IT DOESN'T WANT TO!"

        THE OTHER WOMAN, WHO HAD NOT BEEN SAYING MUCH SPOKE UP THEN. "GOD!
   I'M GLAD IT'S HER AND NOT ME! I WOULD DIE IF SOMETHING THAT BIG WAS SHOVED
   IN ME!"

        TO SAY THAT I WAS GETTING CONCERNED AT THAT POINT WOULD BE AN
   UNDERSTATEMENT. BUT, I WAS READY, SO I THOUGHT TO MYSELF, "LETS GET IT
   OVER WITH!"

        THE LADY OF THE HOUSE TOLD THE OTHER TWO TO PULL MY ASSHOLE OPEN SO
   SHE COULD START IT IN.  I FELT THEIR FINGERS POKE INSIDE ME AND PULL ME
   OPEN. THAT HURT SOME BY ITSELF. THEN I FELT SOMETHING BEING PUSHED AND
   TURNED IN AN EFFORT TO MAKE IT ENTER ME.  I COULD TELL THAT IT WAS BIG AND
   SHE TOLD THEM TO PULL HARDER UNTIL IT STARTED IN.  IT FELT LIKE  THEY WERE
   TEARING ME APART, BUT JUST THEN WHATEVER IT WAS STARTED IN. IT WAS SOME
   RELIEF WHEN THEY GOT THEIR FINGERS OUT, BUT WHATEVER SHE HAD IN HER HAND
   STARTED UP INTO ME.  I COULD FEEL IT STRETCHING ME EVERY INCH OF THE WAY!
   I TRIED TO PULL AWAY FROM HER, BUT SHE KNEW SHE HAD ME WHERE SHE WANTED ME
   AND THAT I WAS GOING TO GET THIS IN ME NO MATTER WHAT.  SHE KEPT PUSHING
   UNTIL I COULD NOT STAND IT ANY MORE AND STARTED TO CRY OUT WITH THE PAIN.
   WITH THE GAG ALL IT AMOUNTED TO WAS FUNNY SOUNDS EVEN AS LOUD AS I TRIED
   TO MAKE IT.  SHE KEPT PUSHING AND PUSHING FOR WHAT SEEMED AN ETERNITY.
   THEN ALL OF A SUDDEN THE AIR THAT WAS INSIDE ME CAME OUT IN A LOW BURPING
   SOUND.  AT THAT POINT SHE PUSHED JUST ONE MORE TIME AND STOPPED.

        "THERE", I HEARD HER SAY, "SHE NOW HAS A ONE AND A QUARTER HOLE UP
   HER ASS.  OK - ONE OF YOU GET THE PAIL FROM THE GARAGE AND THE OTHER GET
   THE WHEEL TRAY WITH THE WATER BOTTLE ON IT OUT OF THE CLOSET."

        IN JUST A MOMENT THEY WERE ALL BEHIND ME AGAIN.  SHE TOOK THE PAIL
   AND PLACED IT ON THE FLOOR BELOW ME.

        "PLUG THIS IN", SHE SAID TO ONE OF THE OTHER WOMEN.

        "PLUG WHAT IN?", I THOUGHT TO MYSELF!

        "HAND ME THAT", SHE SAID, "WE'RE READY TO GIVE HER THE CLEANING OF
   HER LIFE NOW!  TURN THE PUMP ON!"

        PUMP - I KNEW WHAT SHE MEANT THEN, AND IT DIDN'T TAKE BUT A SECOND
   BEFORE I FELT THE TIP OF THE COLONIC TUBE PASS THE OTHER TUBE THAT WAS
   KEEPING ME OPEN.

        SHE SLOWLY PUSHED IT FORWARD AND THEN BACK, WORKING IT IN A LITTLE AT
   A TIME UNTIL I COULD FEEL THE COLONIC TUBE DEEPLY INSIDE ME.  ALL THIS
   TIME THE WATER KEPT RUNNING IN AND OUT OF ME WITH COMPLETE EASE.  SHE KEPT
   MOVING THE COLONIC TUBE IN AND OUT UNTIL ALL THE WATER SHE HAD WAS GONE.

        "NOW, THAT'S WHAT I CALL CLEAN", SHE SAID, SMILING.

        WALKING OVER TO THE DOOR, THE LADY OF THE HOUSE CALLED FOR HER
   HUSBAND TO COME IN.  HE ENTERED AND ASKED WHAT SHE WANTED.

        "HONEY, I WANT YOU TO FASTEN THIS TO THAT HOOK UP  THERE IN THE
   CEILING, SO WE CAN RAISE THIS BITCH UP AND LET HER DRIP DRY", SHE REPLIED.

        "OK LOVE - WHAT EVERY YOU NEED!", HE SAID, FASTENING IT AS SHE HAD
   REQUESTED. "IS THAT ALL YOU NEEDED?", HE ASKED.

        "NO WAIT A MINUTE," SHE SAID, "I MAY NEED YOU TO HELP GET HER HUNG."

        THE TWO OTHER WOMEN RELEASED MY ARMS FROM THE LOWER PART OF THE TABLE
   AND FASTENED THE CUFFS TOGETHER.  THEY DID THEIR BEST TO LIFT ME, BUT WITH
   MY LEGS SO WIDE APART I WAS HARD TO LIFT.

        "WELL, GET OVER HERE AND HELP US!", SHE SAID TO HER HUSBAND.

        "WHERE DO I LIFT FROM?", HE ASKED.

        "I DON'T CARE IF YOU GRAB HER TITS!", SHE SNAPPED, "JUST HELP US GET
   HER UP!"

        WELL, THEY FINALLY GOT ME UP HIGH ENOUGH TO FASTEN MY ARMS TO THE
   CABLE THAT WAS NOW HANGING FROM THE CEILING. AT THAT POINT SHE TOLD HER
   HUSBAND TO PULL ON IT UNTIL I WAS LIFTED UP TO THE CEILING, AND AT THE
   SAME TIME TOLD THE OTHERS TO MOVE THE TABLE OUT OF THE WAY.  GOD,  BEING
   MOVED WITH THAT TUBE INSIDE ME WAS NO JOY, BUT I COULDN'T SAY A THING
   ABOUT IT.  HE LIFTED ME AS HIGH AS SHE WANTED AND FASTENED THE END OFF SO
   I WAS SUSPENDED THERE.

        "OKAY, NOW, LETS ALL GO IN THE LIVING ROOM AND HAVE A FEW DRINKS",
   SHE SAID, "ITS TIME FOR A BREAK!"

        "HOW ABOUT HER?", ONE OF THE OTHER WOMEN ASKED.

        "OH DON'T WORRY ABOUT HER SHE DOESN'T NEED A DRINK, BESIDES SHE HAS
   TO DRIP DRY A WHILE YET", SHE REPLIED.

        AT THAT POINT THEY ALL WENT INTO THE OTHER ROOM AND CLOSED THE DOOR
   BEHIND THEM.  THERE I WAS IN THERE ALONE AND SCARED TO DEATH!  I HAD NO
   IDEA WHAT WAS NEXT!

         ............................................................

       I GUESS I HUNG THERE FOR OVER A HALF HOUR AND NOW THAT TUBE THEY HAD
   LEFT IN ME WAS HURTING LIKE HELL.  IT WAS GETTING TO THE POINT THAT EVERY
   BREATH I TOOK MADE IT HURT, SO I WAS DOING MY BEST TO TAKE SHORT SHALLOW
   BREATHS.  I WAS JUST WISHING THAT SOMEONE WOULD COME IN TO CHECK ON ME FOR
   ANY REASON, JUST COME IN SO I COULD SEE ANOTHER FACE.

       FINALLY, THE DOOR OPENED AND IN CAME EVERYONE THAT WAS IN THE HOUSE.
   THE WOMEN CAME OVER TO ME AND THE MEN SAT IN CHAIRS AROUND THE ROOM.  THE
   WOMAN WHOSE HOME IT WAS DID THE TALKING AT THIS POINT, AND WHAT SHE HAD TO
   SAY I DIDN'T LIKE ONE BIT.  SHE BEGAN BY SAYING HOW SHE FELT ABOUT HER
   HUSBAND AND THAT SHE WANTED TO DO EVERY THING SHE COULD TO SHOW HOW MUCH
   SHE LOVED HIM.  SHE SAID SHE FELT THAT THE OTHERS FELT AS SHE DID AND THAT
   TO PROVE IT THEY WERE GOING TO PUT ON A SHOW FOR THEM.  SHE TOLD THEM TO
   JUST SIT BACK WHILE SHE AND THE OTHERS DEMONSTRATED ALL THEIR FANTASIES
   OUT ON ME.  SHE WANTED THEM TO FULLY ENJOY EVERY MOMENT THAT WAS GOING TO
   TAKE PLACE, AND AT THE SAME TIME SEE WHAT THEIR FANTASIES LOOKED LIKE
   BEING LIVED OUT.

        "READY GIRLS?", SHE ASKED, LOOKING AT THE OTHER WOMEN.

        THEY BOTH NODDED, AND ALL THREE OF THEM BEGAN REMOVING THEIR CLOTHES.
   WHEN THEY HAD REMOVED THEIR OUTER GARMENTS THEY WERE ALL DRESSED ALIKE IN
   BLACK LACE BRAS AND BLACK BIKINI PANTIES.  THEY WERE ALREADY WEARING BLACK
   HIGH HEELS, AND THEY LOOKED LIKE THREE SHE-DEVILS TO ME!

        "OK - FIRST OFF LETS GET THAT TUBE OUT OF HER AND GET HER DOWN FROM
   THERE", SHE INSTRUCTED.

        SHE WALKED UP BEHIND ME AND I COULD FEEL HER GRAB THE END OF THE
   TUBE.  I FELT IT TURN AND THEN SHE PULLED WITH ONE FIRM SOLID JERK.  OUT
   IT CAME AND IT FELT LIKE PART OF ME DID ALSO! I TRIED HARD TO SCREAM, BUT
   IT TURNED OUT TO BE JUST ANOTHER MUFFLED GROAN BECAUSE OF THE GAG.  SHE
   TOLD THE OTHER TWO TO LET MY LEG LOOSE AND ONE OF THEM TO GET THE BAR THAT
   WAS LEANING IN THE CORNER. WHEN MY LEGS WERE LOOSE SHE GOT THE ONE WITH
   THE BAR TO HELP HER FASTEN IT WITH STRAPS BETWEEN MY KNEES.  IT WAS ONLY
   ABOUT THIRTY INCHES OR SO BUT IT WAS ENOUGH TO KEEP MY LEGS APART.  AT
   THAT POINT SHE UNTIED THE ROPE AND LOWERED ME TO THE FLOOR.  GOD, IT FELT
   GOOD TO HAVE MY FEET TOUCH THE FLOOR AGAIN!  SHE DIDN'T LET MY ARMS
   COMPLETELY DOWN, JUST ENOUGH FOR ME TO BE ON THE FLOOR. BUT AFTER HANGING
   THERE FOR THAT LONG I WAS VERY APPRECIATIVE OF EVEN THAT LITTLE BIT.

        LOOKING OVER AT HER HUSBAND, THE LADY OF THE HOUSE ASKED, "HONEY,
   WOULD YOU LIKE TO SEE ANYTHING IN PARTICULAR?"

        "YES", HE SAID, "I'VE ALWAYS WONDERED WHAT IT WOULD BE LIKE IF I HAD
   YOU LIKE THAT  WITH A THIN BELT IN MY HAND WHIPPING YOU UNTIL YOU BEGGED
   FOR ME TO STOP."

        "WHATEVER YOU WANT, HONEY", SHE SAID, "AFTER ALL, THIS IS YOUR NIGHT."

        SHE REACHED UP TO THE BACK OF MY HEAD AND UNFASTENED THE STRAP THAT
   HELD MY GAG.  SHE WALKED OVER TO HER HUSBAND AND TOOK THE BELT HE HAD
   REMOVED AND WALKED BACK TO ME.

   ROOM AGAIN AND THIS TIME SHE WAS GONE A LITTLE LONGER.  WHEN SHE RETURNED
   SHE HAD A LARGE GLASS WATER BOTTLE WITH A MEASURED AMOUNT OF WATER IN IT.
   SHE SAT IT DOWN BEHIND ME NEXT TO THE PUMP.

        "HOW MUCH IS IN IT?", THE THIRD MAN ASKED HER.

        "ONE GALLON AND TWO OUNCES", SHE REPLIED, "PLUS SIX MORE OUNCES  THAT
   THE PUMP WON'T PULL OUT OF THE BOTTLE."

        "OKAY", HE REPLIED, "I'M READY FOR YOU TO GET STARTED."

        WELL AS SHE WAS HOOKING UP THE TUBES BETWEEN THE PUMP AND THE BOTTLE,
   I HEARD THE PUMP START SO SHE COULD RUN THE AIR OUT OF THE LINE SHE WAS
   GOING TO HOOK TO ME. AS SOON AS SHE HAD DONE THAT SHE FASTENED IT TO THE
   MIDDLE TUBE OF THE BARDEX.  I KNEW WHEN SHE HAD, AS I FELT THE WEIGHT OF
   THE ADDED TUBING PULLING ON THE NOZZLE.  THE OTHER TWO WOMEN WERE ALREADY
   OVER WITH THEIR HUSBANDS AND SHE FLIPPED THE SWITCH, STARTING THE PUMP,
   BEFORE JOINING HER HUSBAND.  I COULD FEEL THE WATER COMING IN ME RIGHT
   AWAY AS SHE HAD MADE IT A BIT WARMER THAN I WAS USED TO IT BEING.

       SO THERE I WAS, ALL OF THEM WATCHING ME AS I WAS BEING FILLED TO THE
   LIMIT.  I WAS TRYING TO MOVE A LITTLE SO I COULD ADJUST MYSELF AS THE
   WATER WAS EXPANDING ME.  I COULD SEE THAT THEY WERE LOOKING RIGHT AT MY
   STOMACH AS I KNEW BY THE FEEL THAT I WAS GETTING SOME BIGGER.  THAT WATER
   WAS SLOWLY RUNNING WHEN ONE OF THEM SAID, "WELL, SHE IS HALF WAY NOW."
   "HALF WAY?", I THOUGHT TO MYSELF, "SHIT, I HOPE I'M FARTHER ALONG THAN
   THAT!"

        TIME WAS PASSING SO SLOWLY AND I COULD NOW FEEL THE PRESSURE BUILDING
   MORE AND MORE WITH EACH SECOND THAT PASSED. SOON I WAS STARTING TO REALLY
   HURT AND I STARTED PULLING ON THE ROPES THAT WERE HOLDING ME.  THAT DID NO
   GOOD, BUT JUST MADE ME FEEL THAT MUCH WORSE.  AS THE WATER WAS STILL
   COMING IN ME THE MAN THAT THIS WAS BEING DONE FOR SAID, "WELL, SHE IS ON
   THE LAST QUART NOW."  GOD! I KNEW THAT I COULDN'T HOLD ANY MORE AND I WAS
   BITING MY LIP TRYING TO NOT LET IT GET TO ME. SOON I WAS LETTING OUT LOW
   SOUNDS AS I WASN'T GAGGED THIS TIME, AND BEFORE MUCH MORE TIME HAD PASSED
   I FOUND MYSELF PLEADING WITH THEM.

        "PLEASE TURN THE PUMP OFF", I ASKED.

        "WHY?  YOU AREN'T FULL YET!", THE BITCH REPLIED.

        "PLEASE! PLEASE STOP!...I CAN'T TAKE ANY MORE!", I BEGGED. I KNEW I
   COULD USE MY CODE WORD, BUT I DIDN'T, AND I WAS HOPING THAT I WOULDN'T
   HAVE TO.  I BEGGED THEM AGAIN.

        "PLEASE STOP!!", I SHOUTED THROUGH THE PAIN.

        THEY ALL JUST LOOKED AT ME. I WAS LOOKING PREGNANT NOW AND I COULD
   HEAR THE PUMP JUST RUNNING ON.  FINALLY I HEARD THE FAMILIAR SOUND OF THE
   PUMP RUNNING OUT OF WATER.  I DIDN'T FEEL ANY MORE COMING INTO ME, AND THE
   BITCH GOT UP AND TURNED THE PUMP OFF.

        "HOW LONG CAN WE LEAVE HER FULL LIKE THAT?", THE THIRD MAN ASKED.

        "UNTIL WE LET IT OUT OF HER", THE BITCH REPLIED, SMILING.

        LOOKING AT HIS WATCH, HE REPLIED, "WELL, ITS SIX THIRTY-FIVE NOW.
   LEAVE HER THERE LIKE THAT UNTIL SEVEN AND THEN LET IT OUT OF HER AND LET
   HER DOWN SO SHE CAN BE EMPTIED OUT WHEN HER BROTHER COMES FOR HER."  AND
   THAT IS JUST WHAT SHE DID!

       JUST AT SEVEN SHE PULLED THE TUBE LOOSE AND PLACED IT INTO THE NOW
   EMPTY BOTTLE.  MOST OF WHAT WAS IN ME CAME OUT RIGHT AWAY THROUGH THE
   NOZZLE.  ONCE IT STOPPED SHE HAD THE OTHER TWO WOMEN HELP HER LET ME DOWN
   AND GET ME COMPLETELY UNTIED.  I MEAN SHE TOOK EVERY THING OFF ME, THE
   ROPES AND CUFFS, EVERYTHING.  THE LAST THING SHE DID WAS DEFLATE THE
   BARDEX, BUT THAT ONLY TAKES A SECOND.  ONCE IT WAS OUT SHE TOLD ME TO GET
   UP.  I DID, AS I THOUGHT THAT SHE WAS GOING TO DO SOMETHING ELSE TO ME.

       SHE WALKED AROUND IN FRONT OF ME AND PUT HER ARMS AROUND HE AND HELD
   ME TIGHT TO HER.

        "I KNOW WE'VE HURT YOU.  I HOPE YOU AREN'T MAD AT US", SHE SAID
   WARMLY.

        "NO", I REPLIED, HONESTLY, "IN MY OWN WAY I ENJOYED IT TOO, PROBABLY
   AS MUCH AS ALL OF YOU DID."

        "WELL, I COULDN'T BLAME YOU IF YOU NEVER WANTED TO COME BACK TO OUR
   HOME AGAIN", SHE SAID.

        "OF COURSE I WANT TO COME BACK", I SAID SINCERELY. "WHY WOULD YOU
   THINK I WOULDN'T WANT TO?"

        "BECAUSE OF ALL THE THINGS WE MADE YOU DO", SHE REPLIED.

        "ITS OKAY", I SAID.  "I REALLY DIDN'T MIND IT AT ALL, AND EVEN AS
   SORE AS I AM, I'LL GET OVER IT BEFORE LONG.

        "GOD, YOU'RE A GOOD SPORT ABOUT IT", SHE SAID. "ANY TIME WE CAN DO
   ANYTHING FOR YOU, PLEASE JUST ASK."

        AT THAT POINT I NEED TO GO RELIEVE MYSELF SOME MORE, AND I ASKED TO
   LEAVE.

        "OKAY", SHE ANSWERED, "BUT IF YOU HAVE ANY PROBLEMS LET US KNOW!"

        "SURE THING!", I REPLIED.

        "WELL, YOU CAN WAIT ANYWHERE YOU LIKE UNTIL YOUR BROTHER GETS HERE,
   AND BE SURE TO TELL HIM FOR ME JUST HOW GOOD YOU WERE", SHE SAID.

        I TOLD HER I WOULD AND LEFT FOR THE NEAREST BATHROOM. WHEN I GOT
   FINISHED CLEANING UP AND CAME BACK INTO THE LIVING ROOM THEY WERE ALL
   GONE.  I SUSPECTED THAT THEY HAD HEADED OFF TO SEPARATE BEDROOMS FOR SOME
   SLEEP.  I KNEW AS SOON AS MY RIDE CAME THAT WAS JUST WHAT I WAS GOING TO
   DO MYSELF.

       THIS IS THE END OF MY REPORT, AS MY EVENINGS WITH THEM ARE ALWAYS ONE
   TIME THINGS.  SO UNTIL THE NEXT TIME....
