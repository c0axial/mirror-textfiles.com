MALE OR FEMALE.  THE WORD "SEX" HAS MANY DERIVATIVES.  BELOW IS A SENTENCE OF

I HAVE GIVEN STEP-BY-STEP INSTRUCTIONS IN HOW TO ENGAGE IN IT.	READ CAREFULLY,
YOU JUST MIGHT LEARN SOMETHING!!!

OR A LESBIAN), AND ASK HIM (OR HER IF SHE (OR HE) WOULD LIKE TO HAVE "SEX".  IF
SHE (OR HE) SAYS OK, YOU'RE IN LUCK, OTHERWISE TOO BAD!!!

IS UP TO YOU!).  THEN ENGAGE IN SOME "SEXUAL" FOREPLAY (MEANING MAKING OUT, OR
WHAT EVER YOU LIKE.).

STEP 3:
 IF YOU'RE TURNED ON, STRIP!!!  IF NOT, CONTINUE WITH THE FOREPLAY.  ONCE YOU
ARE STRIPPED, GET INTO A BED, OR SOME OTHER COMFORTABLE SPOT.

STEP 4:
 NOW, ASSUME THE BOY-ON-GIRL (OR GIRL-ON-BOY) POSITION, AND GET COMFORTABLE.

INTO TARGET AREA "X".  GIRLS, MAKE SURE YOUR MEN DO THIS SLOWLY, AS TO ATTAIN
MAXIMUM PLEASURE!

STEP 5:
 HAVE THE PERSON ON TOP, GIRL OR BOY, START MAKING QUICK UP-AND-DOWN THRUSTS.
BY THE WAY, THE BOY-ON-GIRL POSITION IS PREFERABLE, BECAUSE THERE WILL BE MORE
POWER BEHIND EACH STROKE.  AFTER A FEW SECONDS, BOTH PARTNERS WILL BEGIN TO
EXPERIENCE GREAT FEELINGS OF "EUPHORIA" (SEE DEFINITION BELOW).  CONTINUE TO DO
THIS, AND YOU WILL COME TO WHAT IS KNOWN AS THE "CLIMAX", WHERE YOU WILL GET AN
"ORGASM" (SEE DEFINITIONS BELOW), AND YOU WILL EXPERIENCE TOTALLY AWESOME
EUPHORIA!!  (BY THE WAY, DURING THE ENTIRE TIME OF FORWARD-THRUSTS, YOU ARE
STILL RUBBING EACH OTHERS BODY!)

STEP 6:
 THANK YOUR PARTNER FOR THE GREAT TIME, AND SMOKE A CIGARRETTE (IF YOU DON'T
SMOKE, DRINK A PEPSI!) HAVE FUN!!

EUPHORIA- A STATE OF TOTAL PLEASURE OR GREAT FEELING.
