Subject: The List of Everything Warner Animation

Here is a list of all of the cartoons which Warner Animation has ever
released. (and a few that they haven't) I have also put a remark for all
of the ones which are available on LD or tape, as far as I know.

I have included all of the classic LT/MM cartoons, as well as all of
TTA (including each different version of each TTA cartoon), and BTAS,
Taz-Mania, and all of Animaniacs up to three weeks ago. I'll post a
more complete list for Animaniacs at the end of the week.

Everything is listed in chronological order, except for the first season
of Taz-Mania, which I don't have air dates for.

Please report any errors or omissions. This list may be freely copied.

================================================================================
534 MM = Merrie Melodies
468 LT = Looney Tunes
 26 PS = Private Snafu
 19 TS = TV Special
 11 SS = Special Short
  9 RT = Re-released: Originally part of TV Special
  6 FC = Feature Length Compilation
  5 L? = Looney Tunes or Merrie Melodies
  4 LA = Looney Tunes characters in part of a live action movie
  1 CS = Cartoon Special
205 TT = Tiny Toon Adventures
121 TM = Taz-Mania
 65 BT = Batman: The Animated Series
 63 AN = Animaniacs
