Spell schools are included for specialist wizards.  Those 
spells listed with two or more schools separated by a "/" can 
be cast by a specialist with access to EITHER school (ie, the 
spell "Tymessul's Cerulean Traverse", which is listed as 
"Alt/Ench", can be cast by any wizard with access to either the 
Alteration OR Enchantment schools, or both).  Spells listed 
with schools separated by a "," can be cast only by mages with 
access to BOTH schools (ie, "Succor", listed as "Alt, Ench", 
can be cast only be mages with access to Alteration AND 
Enchantment magicks).
