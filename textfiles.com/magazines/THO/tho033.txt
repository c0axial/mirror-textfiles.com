H0W T0 MAKE A YUMMY SNACK

  A pansy anarchist gets hungry while causing their own form of chaos.  This
is why I am releasing my very own favorite recipie for on the go snacking.
You can make this using ingredients found all over mom's kitchen.  Little 
does she know that she's helping you with your chaos.


T0 MAKE A YUMMY SNACK, you'll need:

¯ 1 cup of butter or margarine, softened
¯ 1 cup of granulated sugar
¯ 1/2 cup brown sugar, firmly packed
¯ 2 eggs
¯ 1 and 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
¯ 2 and 1/4 cups of flour
¯ 1 teaspoon of baking soda
¯ 1/2 teaspoon of salt
¯ 12 oz of semi-sweet chocolate chips
¯ 1 cup of chopped nuts (optional)


HERE'S WHAT Y0U D0:

  Beat butter and sugars together until fluffy.  Beat in eggs, one at a
time;  stir in vanilla.  Gradually stir in flour, baking soda and salt.
Add chocolate chips and nuts (add 'em if you got 'em!)  Drop mixture by
teaspoonfills onto lightly greased cookie sheets.  Beak in preheated
375øF oven for eight to ten minutes until golden brown.  This recipie
makes about a dozen snack units.
