       Soap: 3-4 cups of animal fat
             1 five pound size can
             Filter cloth
             Heating device
             Water
             Lye
             2 Metal bowls
             Rose oil

          1: Heat the fat on the kitchen stove 'till it liquifies.
          2: Pour it through the filter cloth into the can. Discard remaing fat.
             Repeat step 2 until the can is full.
          3: Put 4 cups of water into the bowl, add 10 ounces of powdered lye.
             Stir slowly. Avoid hand and eye contact.
          4: Heat the solution to 100 degrees fahrenheit. And remove from heat.
          5: Add the can 'o fat to a second bowl. Heat to 130 fahrenheit.
          6: Add the melted fat to the lye solution. Stop pouring when it 
             reaches the consistency of pancake syrup.
          7: Add a few drops of the rose oil to it.
          8: Pour it into a bar mold.
          9: Let it cure for 1 week.

  Deodorant: 16 ounce pump spray bottle
             Isopropyl Alcohol
             Alum
             Zinc Oxide

          1: Pour 1 cup water in bottle.
          2: Add 1/4 cup of alcohol to bottle.
          3: Stir in 1/2 tablespoon of zinc oxide.

 Toothpaste: 1 Baby food jar
             Salt
             Baking Soda
             Glycerin

          1: Put 1/4 cup of salt in bottle.
          2: Add 1/4 cup of baking soda.
          3: Shake well, with cap on.
          4: Add (one at a time) six teaspoons of glycerin.
          5: Add water 'till it turns to paste.

Hand Lotion: Squeeze Bottle
             Gelatin
             Glycerin
             Rose Oil

          1: Dissolve 2 teaspoons of gelatin in 1 cup hot water.
          2: Let it cool.
          3: Add 2 tablespoons 'o glycerin.
          4: Mix in some rose oil for fragrance.
