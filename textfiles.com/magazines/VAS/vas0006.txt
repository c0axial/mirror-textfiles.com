        Hello boys and girls.  I am assuming that you live in the Metro
Detroit area.  If not, you might as well stop reading this right now.  The
authors of V.a.S. discovered an interesting little 'graveyard'.  It contains
about 15 to 20 planes, that have been stripped out.  It appears they have
been untouched for some time, although we cannot verify this information.
We do not know where these planes originated, but we also have several theo-
ries about where they came from.  This first, and most likely theory, invol-
ves some old geezer that likes to collect planes.  The second theory has to
do with a major plane crash, which involves like 20 planes crashing into each
other in midair at the same time.  This theory seems unlikely, but we are not
ruling it out quite yet.  The third theory is even more outrageous, and it
suggests that the planes were taken down by some mysterious force, such as
the bermuda triangle.  Anyways, most of the planes there are Cessna's and
other small single engine planes.  They are just about all stripped out, with
some of them still retaining switches and gauges on the dash.  This area is
located behind Meijer's on 8 mile and Haggerty.  Please obey all the posted
trespassing signs (ha ha, what a joke) and watch out for a low-lying ( 1 foot
high) rusted barbwired fence.  Other then that, if you are board, or need
some place to go, I would recommend you check out the plane graveyard.  You
may think this t-file was lame, but that's just because yer a geek.  Future
issues of V.A.S. will include actual fiction stories tastefully arranged with
the technical aspects of hacking etc.  I feel that this enhances the reader's
pleasure by offering a funny story to stimulate the creative side of the
reader's brain.  Oh well, if you speak japanese, call 18003451517.
