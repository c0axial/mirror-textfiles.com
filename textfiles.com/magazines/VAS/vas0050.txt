º                            The History Of VaS.                              º
ÓÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄÄ½

        May 12, 1992 was the historic date that VaS was founded. It all
started a long long time ago, when Phreak_accident and I had a yearning to
start a textfile group, to "leave our mark" in the BBS world as such groups
as uXu and cDc had done. Anyhow, here we are at issue #50 after less than 2
months, thanks to the contributions of our authors. Some of our earlier
efforts at textfile writing were USA (Underground Society of Anarchy), which
we wrote 1 magazine, mostly reprints of other tfiles reworded a bit. That
flopped. Then we tried something called ALPHA (American Legion of Phreakers,
Hackers, and Anarchists) but after 2 issues, that flopped as well. So I went
into writing a few textfiles on the side, but nothign serious. Then me and
P_A started to get more and more time on our hands, and finally we decided one
last try, if this doesnt work, fuck it. But we decieded to really put some
effort into it. After a few phone conversations, we couldnt come up with a
name, I had suggested countless stupid ones, and finally he said, hey, hows
about Vaginal and Anal Seepage. I said sure, that sounds pretty cool. So there
we were. You may be saying "Seepage! SEEPAGE! What The Fuck Its -SECRETIONS-!".
Ahh therein lies the story. I have a poor short term memory, so I decided, ok
I guess it was secretions, so thats what I made it, and thats how it ender.
        We would like to thank the following people for their work so far on
VaS: Me, Phreak_Accident, Hacker, Probe-X, and Nocturnus. Also would like to
thank our Distribution sites <we need new ones though - contact Studmuffin
on Live Wire BBS to find out about becoming one - no more 313's and you must
have a lot of textfiles to begin with!>. FLoaTiNG PaNCReaS BBS is a fine BBS,
has thousands upon thousands of great tfiles, and a great sysop. We are gonna
try and get some kind of network going between all the big major tfile boards
soon...
        Anyhow, here we are at issue #50. I personally never thought we would
make it this far. I hope we keep on going to issue #50,000 myself, its so
much fun. Granted lots of the issues are really stupid and disgusting, but
oh well. Anyhow, after a few calculations on our Distribution sites, the 3
most popular issues of VaS so far have been:

                      #1 - Basic Rape Techniques.
                      #2 - A Guide To Lamerz.
                      #3 - The Cannibal's Cookbook.

        And please remember we ALWAYS need more authors here at VaS. If you
can contribute: News articles on computer-underground-related topics, virii
sources that YOU have written, Album lyrics, bomb ideas, anarchy ideas,
electronics plans, or anything offensive or outrageous, just send it up to
LiVe WiRe BBS and i will get it all set up for you. Then YOUR name can appear
in VaS #100 (if we make it that far - although we are putting them out at a
pretty steady pace).
        Anyhow I know lots of them are lame, but oh well. Everything else has
already been written on.
