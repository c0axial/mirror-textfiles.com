     INFORMATION ON GENIE
     --------------------

     ACTUALLY CALLED 'GENIE', WHICH IS AN ACRONYM FOR 'GENERAL ELECTRIC
NETWORK FOR INFORMATION EXCHANGE'.
     GENIE IS GE'S REPLY TO COMPUSERVE. IT'S PRETTY MUCH THE SAME AS
COMPUSERVE; IT HAS SIG'S, BBS'S, A CB SIMULATOR, A MAIL SYSTEM,
VARIOUS NEWSLETTERS, MAGAZINES, AN ONLINE ENCYCLOPEDIA, AND ON-LINE GAMES.
     IT EVEN USES 3 LETTER COMMANDS FOR IT'S MENUES, JUST LIKE CPS.
(LIKE 'CAT' FOR 'CATEGORIES').  IT EVEN DIPLICATES THE 'PAGES' THE CPS OFFERS,
THE FORMAT FOR THE COMMAND ON GENIE IS 'M XXX', WHERE XXX IS THE PAGE
NUMBER.
     THE SIG'S ON GENIE (CALLED ROUNDTABLES), ARE A LOT MORE SPECIFIC
THAN ON COMPU-RIP, BUT THEY STILL SERVE THE SAME PURPOSE.  THERE ARE
ROUNDTABLES FOR GAMES, COMPUTERS, SCIENCE FICTION AND SCIENCE FANTASY
AND FOR BBSES, WHERE YOU CAN TRANSFER PROGRAMS UNDER THAT TOPIC.

     THE LARGEST VARIATION OCCURS IN THE AREA OF CHARGES.  GENIE IS
$ 1.25 CHEAPER DURING NON-PRIME TIME, THIS IS ACCOMPLISHED BY GENIE CHARGING
$ 35.00 DURING PRIME TIME.

     ALSO, UPLOADING, WHICH IS DONE IN XMODEM, IS FREE. DOWNLOADING WHICH

     CALLING GENIE
     -------------

     SINCE YOU CANNOT USE GATEWAYS TO ACCESS GENIE, GENIE HAS SET UP THEIR

     HACKING GENIE
     -------------

     IT SEEMS THAT THE LONGEST PLANNING TIME FOR GENIE WENT INTO THE SECURITY.
     HACKING GENIE IS DEFINITELY A CHORE, AND ANYONE WHO SUCCEEDS
WINS MY INSTANT RESPECT. (ONLY IF YOU SEND ME THE INFO THAT YOU USED)
     HACKING GENIE IS TOUGH BECAUSE ALTHOUGH YOU CAN CALL UP THEIR 800
TRY-OUT LINE, YOU CANNOT JUST LIE ABOUT YOUR CHECKING ACCOUNT AND GAIN
ACCESS UNTIL SOMEONE FINDS OUT.  THE REASON IS BECAUSE ALTHOUGH YOU GIVE
ALL YOUR NORMAL INFORMATION (YOUR ADDRESS, YOUR NAME, YOUR
TELEPHONE NUMBER, YOUR CREDIT CARD NUMBER)...YOU ALSO GIVE YOUR
MOTHER'S MAIDEN NAME, AND HOURS TO CALL YOU.  THEN A FEW DAYS
LATER, A GENIE OPERATOR CALLS YOU AND TELLS YOU YOUR PASSWORD, AND
YOUR MAILBOX NAME. PASSWORDS ARE ENTERED IN THE

ID#,PASS [RETURN]

     FORMAT.  YOUR ID# IS ACTUALLY 8 DIGITS AND LETTERS COMBINED,
EX. 'SEX12345'.  (DOESN'T HAVE TO HAVE A WORD IN IT, I JUST PULLED THAT ONE
OFF THE TIP OF MY TONGUE.)
     AND YOUR PASSWORD IS ANY WORD OR COMBINATION OF DIGITS AND LETTERS
THAT YOU CAN THINK OF. EX. 'SNOWBUNNY' SO THAT THE ENTIRE
CODE LOOKS LIKE THIS:

SEX12345,SNOWBUNNY [RETURN]

     MAKE SURE THAT THERE ARE NO SPACES IN THE ID,PASSWORD COMBO,
(OR BETWEEN THE TWO) BECAUSE IF THERE ARE, THEY WILL BE COUNTED AS PART OF
THE PASSWORD.
     SO GUESSING SOMEONE'S ID&PASSWORD IS GOING TO BE PRETTY TRICKY.
     GENIE HAS AN 800 NUMBER SET UP RIGHT NOW, THAT ALLOWS YOU TO CALL
UP AND SIGN ON.  JUST CALL THE NUMBER, WHICH IS GIVEN AT THE END
OF THE FILE, AND WHEN YOU ARE CONNECTED, ENTER :

"HHH" [RETURN]
"5JM11988,GENIE" [RETURN]

     IT WILL TELL YOU THAT THE SERVICE YOU ARE USING IS NO LONGER AVAILABLE,
AND WILL TELL YOU THAT THE START-UP RATE IS $ 18.00, THEN IT WILL ASK YOU 
IF YOU WANT TO START UP.  ENTER "Y", AND THEN YOU CAN GET A LIST OF
LOCAL NUMBERS TO HACK ON.
