        journal of william ernest nuremberg
        first entry
        december 8 1989

        hello.  to anyone who might be reading this, i greet you.  at
 this point you are either well-aware of my identity or completely
 clueless as to the origin of this journal.  i would hope it to be the
 latter, but i do not think that is any of my business at this time.  i
 suppose it would be most prudent to simply explain who i am.  i am
 william nuremberg, m.d., and i am a big stupid jerk.  it is for this
 reason that i have decided to terminate my life.

        goodbye.
