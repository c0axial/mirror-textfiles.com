By Roz


We The People

Pressed between their fingers
Thrown against the wall
My life, my future, my will
A testimony to all

Hurled into a dead society
I am but a shred of doubt
Life's dignity is brightly lit
But the candle's going out

I realize the wrongful truth
Life's lessons can only teach
Poor damned souls newly wrought
Bleached bones lay upon the beach


And with ever prayer and wish
I'm afraid none can be sent
For exposed and naked we all are
Our sins will be our end.




Mythical Advice

Dance little one,
til the end of the road.
Spook no man's soul,
for the fortunate are gold.
Dance until you sweat,
on a pretty pink dress.
Cry til you hush,
sodden your delicate flesh.

-Roz
