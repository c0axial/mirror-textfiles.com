I Carry
By MurphDog

I carry. Towards the end of the year, people tend to carry less. It's a
time to relax, summer time. I carry more. The temperture runs high, and
so do tempers. You never know what to expect these days. I carry more for
a reason. I'm a good kid. I live in a good neighborhood. I have no
problems at home. I carry more for a reason, which is... These kids
frighten me. Coming home beaten to a pulp, but never again. I carry more
for a reason, which is, I'm scared.
