To Stephen After the Storm
by Utahna Faith

Where are you my friend
My tall ambling buddy
Colored for the cold and living in the heat?
Where are you with your
Sun lemon hair and baby sky eyes
Dancing with learned wisdom
And a certain innocence so rare.

Come back from the storm
Back from the third world
From the wild turns of the tropics, be safe.

Find your way back, sit across from me, tell me:
"Two dogs walk into a bar."
