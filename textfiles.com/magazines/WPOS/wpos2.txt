A letter to the establishment
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

To all governments in the world who have made Weed illegal,

What valid excuse do you have to outlaw the cultivating, harvesting and smoking
of Weed? In the UK and most of the other Western countries it seems you have
more dangerous drugs that are legally available such as tobacco and alcohol.

The most sickening fact I recently found out about the UK tobacco industry is
that the Health Service receives donations from them. When smokers suffer from
heart and lung diseases, the Health Service simply patches them up and sends
them back to the tobacco industry good as new. And alcohol...

Did you know you can even die from alcohol without even touching a sip? How I
hear you ask? You could be beaten, stabbed, or shot by a drunken maniac.

As a fact of the matter is that Weed acts as a pacifier and uplifter. And maybe
if the Health Service and the Medical Establishment accepted how Hemp is very
beneficial to people's health the world would be a better place. There are many
Multiple Sclerosis sufferers who use hash to relieve their symptoms, which are
often very painful and disruptive to their daily lives.

Quoting an article written by John Bentley for the Hemp Magazine :
"And I ask just one question: how many average members of the public are aware
of the fact hemp is capable of being the number one energy resource on this
planet, causing no pollution, no toxic wastage, and at the same time helping
slow down the Greenhouse Effect?"

So please how can you justify yourselves? Are you scared that we'll enjoy
ourselves too much instead of being miserable because everyday life is so
boring?

Yours Truly

BooYaa

p.s. I'd like to commend those European countries which have legalized Weed as
it seems their governments have their heads screwed on the right way.

p.s.s No quotes for you because I didn't think you were worthy.
