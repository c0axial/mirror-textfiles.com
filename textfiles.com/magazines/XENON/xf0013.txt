   Since the Internet was originally created by the governmnet, as ARPANET,
   one  would expect  there to be many government and military connections.
   What  one may  be surprised of,  however, is the number of corporations,
   universities,  and other  organizations  that are doing projects for the
   United  States  Government  and Military.  Below is an extensive listing
   of  the sites  on the  Internet that are  known to be involved with this
   type  of  work.  Names  like  Xerox,  Digital Equipment Corporation, and 
   MIT,  are  amoung  the  few  corporations  and  universities employed by
   the  government to do research  and/or projects; usually relating to the 
   US Military in some way. Be warned, however, that the following machines 
   are not as secure as one may be led to believe; a moderate hacker may be 
   able  to  access  dozens  from  the  list.  The  penalties for illegally 
   accessing  any one  of these computers usually involves imprisonment and 
   stiff fines.  With that warning, let us begin...
