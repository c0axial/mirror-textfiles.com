Mortal Kombat Condensed Moves List
----------------------------------
Liu Kang
--------
Fireball: F-F-HP
Flying Kick:  F-F-HK
Fatality:  Full Circle Away (Hold BLOCK)

Johnny Cage
-----------
Green Bolt:  B-F+LP or B-F-LP
Shadow Kick:  B-F+LK or B-F-LK
Ball Breaker:  BLOCK+LP
Fatality:  F-F-F-HP

Kano
----
Knife Throw:  B-F(Hold BLOCK)
Body Ball:  3/4 Circle Away (Start from F.  Hold BLOCK)
Fatality:  Half-Circle Toward + LP or B-D-F+LP or B-D-F-LP

Raiden
------
Lightning:  Half-circle Toward + LP
Body Launch:  B-B-F
Teleport:  Center-D-U-Center (Flick joystick down)
Fatality:  F-B-B-B + HP

Scorpion
--------
Spear:  B-B-LP
Teleport Punch:  Half-circle away + HP
Fatality:  U-U (Hold BLOCK)

Sub Zero
--------
Freeze:  Half-circle toward + LP
Slide:  B + LP + LK + BLOCK
Fatality:  F-D-F-HP or F-Half-circle toward-HP

Sonya
-----
Ring Toss:  LP - B+LP
Flying Punch:  F-B+HP or F-B-HP
Leg Throw:  LP + LK + BLOCK
Fatality:  F - F - B - B - BLOCK
