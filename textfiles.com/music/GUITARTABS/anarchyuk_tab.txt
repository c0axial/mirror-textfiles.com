This is how I play it, not necessarily the "correct" way. The song is fairly
simple, involving basically only one power chord shape.  Use heavy distortion.
You also might like to mute the bass E string with your thumb.

Forgive me for missing some of the lyrics, part of the solo, and the
little embellishments from the original, since I haven't heard the
original in a while and I never learned it all anyway.
