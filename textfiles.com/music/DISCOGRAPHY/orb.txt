+->        (i)  Abbreviations
 
                 7"    -  seven inch single
                 12"   -  twelve inch single
                 PS    -  picture sleeve (original issue)
                 Pr    -  promotional copy
                 WL    -  white label promo copy
                 LP    -  album, long playing vinyl format
                 MC    -  album, music cassette format
                 CD    -  album, compact disc format
                 UK    -  United Kingdom release
                 US    -  United States release
 
 
          (ii)  Catalogue number prefixes
 
                 MWS, APOLLO     -  WAU!MrModo Records
                 BLR, ORB PROMO  -  WAU!MrModo through Big Life
 
                 Other labels are named, where known.
 
 
         (iii)  Historical Note
 
                 The Orb was created in late 88/early 89 by Alex
                 Paterson  of  EG Records  and  WAU!MrModo,  and
                 Jimmy Cauty from KLF Communications, after they
                 DJ'd several  'ambient'  club nights  together.
                 After  two  records,  The Orb  was  temporarily
                 disbanded,  aborting the release of their debut
                 album  'Space'  on  KLF  Comunications,  and  a
                 planned triple  album.  Alex later resumed work
                 as The Orb,  but  this  time  with  Thrash from
                 WAU!MrModo,  while Jimmy continued working with
                 Bill Drummond as The KLF.
