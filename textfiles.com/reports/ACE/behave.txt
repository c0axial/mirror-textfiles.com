					  THROWING THINGS IN A CLASSROOM

	 Throwing things in a classroom should not be done for many reasons.  A
few reasons that i would like to add that lead me to beleive this fact is
because I have been a victem of foreign objects flying in my face. When i
was younger however i threw things about the class room such as spitballs,
paper , books , tinfoil , sandwhiches , apples , tennis balls ect...As I
grew older and more mature i realized that throwing things is a sign of
immaturity. How would you as a victem feel if you were the center of
soemones amusment getting a kick out of throwing metal in your nostrils ?
If you can't relate to such a situation I will tell you a little story...

Goon starting throwing pennys around the room. Now Jonny was a smart boy ,
while he was connecting his scr transistor across his 300 amp  1200 volt
power supply a penny came flying across the room into his glasses then onto
his transistor,  Jon looked up , about to cry , he said " thanks alot Goon
" , And continued working . Now Jon was working for about 1/2 an hour , and
his 1200 volt invention is now 3000 volt invention with not one scr but 20,
his resistors were just about all plugged into the pc board when Goon
through another penny at him. Jonny was still testing his project when .
ZAP ! Jonny melted onto his ` project because the penny by-passed the
swithing board in the circuit and Jonny got 3000 volts between his head .
Goon didn't care , He just wanted his penny back .
