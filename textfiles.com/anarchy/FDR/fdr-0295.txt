    and a fuse (read: a stiffed wax-rope).  Now, systematically, roll the
    the pieces of toilet-paper thru the wax and put immediatly on the
    lighter.  Don't put *too* much paper on the damn thing, but let's say
    just a few (4-papers).  Now, attach the fuse onto the lighter with
    some leftover-wax...  Let everything dry...

    ...and you have a nice little bomb!!  Light the fuse, put it down on
    the ground and run!
