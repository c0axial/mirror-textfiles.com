	SysOp's now you can get BBS Callers Digest OnLine Version!

	BBS Callers Digest is the premier printed BBS related Magazine, the
Online version is an excellent asset to your BBS System.  BBS Callers Digest
Online gives you and your users access to timely computer industry news,
technology related wirestories, monthly columns like Uncle Hank's
Shareware Review and features stories about the movers and shakers in the
BBSing world, plus a special online classified advertising section.

	Pics OnLine! has entered into an exclusive arrangement to offer you BBS
Callers Digest OnLine, each month you will receive the latest issue of BBS
Callers Digest at the same time they are mailed to the subscribers.  

	We have prepared a special door program for displaying BBS Callers
Digest for you and you users, this program is provided to you free with
your first year's subscription.  The Program supports the following BBS
Types: PCBoard, Wildcat!, Gap, QBBS, RBBS, Remote Access, SpitFire, Ultra,
WWIV, plus any other BBS software that supports any of the above methods
of calling a door.  If you system will not support a door, special
arrangements may be possible, Please contact our office for more
information.

Pricing Structure:

BBS Callers Digest Online is available via a variety of different methods.
Subscribing Sysops may either call Pics OnLine! (PCBoard) as a user a pick
up each month's issue, or as a Qmail Service through an automated script
or it is available as a Fidonet File Hold.  Special Arrangements may also
be available to have it sent to you as a uucp mail message.

The Pricing of BBS Callers Digest Online is on a per node basis, a node is
defined as a phone line connected to the bbs for use by callers, if the
system using X.25 or some other networking protocol were virtual circuits
are used, special arrangements will be made.
