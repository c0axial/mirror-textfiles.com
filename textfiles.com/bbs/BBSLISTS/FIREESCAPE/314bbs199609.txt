LEGEND #3: Board Status Flags 'STATS'
-------------------------------------
* = No Answer.
N = New or Returning BBS.
R = Offers RIPscrip Graphical Emulation.
C = Requires a NUP [New User Password]. (New users by invitation only)
A = Available only during Specific Hours. (NOT 24 HOURS, just part-time)
P = Problems connecting or with modem negotiation etc..
I = Full or Partial Internet Access Available.
$ = Pay Board. (Requires a $ donation or subscription)
? = BBS is alternately busy or not answering. Status UNSURE.

LEGEND #4: Hours of Operation 'HOURS'
--------------------------------------
All BBS Hours are listed in Military 24-Hour Time where Midnight to Noon is
displayed as 00 through 12 and Noon to 11PM is displayed as 12 through 22.
Boards which are up around the clock (with the sole exception of netmail and
or nitely maintainence) are listed as simply "24" which stands for 24 Hours
a Day. Boards which open or close on the HALF-hour will have their time of
opening or closing rounded up or down to the nearest Hour BEFORE the exact
closing time or AFTER the exact opening time to prevent untimely callers.

PLEASE BE COURTEOUS TO NON-24 HOUR BBSES AND CHECK THE TIME BEFORE YOU CALL!
