        To obtain The Garden State BBS List from any of the listed
        distribution sites, either download  GSBBSxxx.??? (xxx=issue
        number) from the BBS, or File Request GSBBS.

        Also, any FidoNet, JerseyNet or HSAnet SysOp can  get The Garden
        State BBS List directly from my system every month.  Just write
        me NetMail about it, and it'll be automatically put on hold for
        you on the 1st of every month.  Just poll me any time after midnight
        (that day or later).

        The Garden State BBS list is automatically posted (UUENCODED) on
        the first of every month  in the ALT.BBS.LISTS Internet Newsgroup.
        If you'd like it posted in another echo where it wouldn't be off
        topic (or rude) to post it every month, let me  know and I'll see
        what I can do.

        You can now get The Garden State BBS List from 2 File Distribution
        Networks as well, if you are a Member of JerseyNet the FileEcho Tag
        is JDS_BBSL or if you are a FidoNet Member the FileEcho Tag is
        BBSL_ST.

        If you can't get the list any of the above ways, write me at any of
        my listed  addresses.
