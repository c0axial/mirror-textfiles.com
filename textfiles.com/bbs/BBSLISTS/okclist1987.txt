This is a listing of the Bulletin
Board Service in the Oklahoma City
area.   Each phone number was checked
and confirmed  as of September 1, 1987
This listing will be checked monthly
to re- confirm the numbers. If you are
a Sysop of a BBS and would like your
number added to this list,please
contact Fido of Moore BBS and leave a
comment to the Sysop.  If you are
removing your BBS from public
service,  please let us know  so that
we can remove your number off this
list. Some of these boards are  NOT
24 hours and may only be reached
during evenings or weekends. Those
names  with a splat  (*)  in front of
the name  indicates some kind of
change in the information since last
month's list. 

[a=Atari, A=Apple, C=Commodore, I=IBM,
T=Tandy, t=TI]
