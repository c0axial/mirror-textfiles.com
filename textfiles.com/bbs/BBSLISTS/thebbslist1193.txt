=KEY/INTERPRETATIONS/IMPLICATIONS/MISCELLANEOUS=

 9HST = 9600HST; 1HST = 14.4HST/16.8HST; 3HST = 38.4HST; HST = ?HST
 $ = Pay subscription optional; $$ = Pay subscription required
 G = Gigabyte
 Msgs = Messages; Adt = Adult; OLGs = Online games

Any modem 9600 or faster is v32 or Dual Standard unless otherwise listed.
Any storage note marked not marked G is in megabytes.
Removable storage (i.e. tape drives, CD-ROMs, etc.) is noted separately
 with "[Media] [Capacity] ([Mounted Simultaneously])", ex. Tape 45(2).
Net Addresses are semi-colon delimited in this order:
 InterNet;uucp;FidoNet
SysOps' Net Addresses are the name/number that he/she can be reached at
 at the particular address. They are in the same sequence as addresses.
The VERIFIED field contains the month/year the BBS was last verified or
 first listed.
NOTE FOR CALLING OUTSIDE-USA: Don't forget your ISD access code before
the phone numbers. Any phone numbers not in xxx-xxx-xxxx format are
located outside the US and Canada.
