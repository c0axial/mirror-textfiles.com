 General Information
 -------------------

 The Pasco BBS Magazine is a monthly magazine which is distributed free of
 charge, as long as it is unaltered and complete.  When uploading make sure
 the original archive is intact with all files included.  The files are
 listed in FILES.LST.

                             +   +   +   +   +

 How to Help
 -----------

 If you would like to write something for the Pasco BBS magazine, please
 send it along.  The more who contribute, the better the magazine will
 become.  All authors will receive a by-line in the Pasco BBS Magazine.
 Sysops or local businesses may receive a free ad for their efforts.  Game
 reviews, articles, computer related humor, questions, general feedback and
 artwork would be greatly appreciated.

 Anyone who would like to have their software, network, or whatever covered
 in the magazine is encouraged to get in touch.

 Uploading the Pasco BBS Magazine to other boards is greatly appreciated.

                             +   +   +   +   +

 Ad Information
 --------------

 At the present time the only way to get an ad is to help with the magazine
 in some way.  If you write something for the magazine you will receive at
 least one free ad.  Distribution Sites also receive free ads.  It is
 preferred that ads for the magazine not exceed 23 lines.

 Advertising space is limited, so the editor makes all determinations on
 which ads will run.  Ads should be forwarded to the Board of Trade BBS.  The
 Pasco BBS Magazine cannot be held responsible for information contained
 within ads.  All ads remain the property of their owners.

 Distribution Site Information
 -----------------------------

 Sysops interested in becoming a Pasco BBS Magazine Distribution Site
 please read the file PBMDIST.INF for all the details.  It is now easier than
